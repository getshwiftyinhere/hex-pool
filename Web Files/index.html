 What's HEXPOOL?

HEXPOOL enables anyone to benefit from the Bigger Pays Better(BPB) HEX staking bonus!
See the verified contract code here.

 Pool HEX funds together as one stake to maximize gains!
 Pools are staked automatically upon reaching a threshold of 150M HEX(MAX BPB), any overflow funds are rolled over into a new pool.
 Each pool has a set stake length of 36, 365 or 3650 days(MAX LPB), enter any and all pools as many times as you like.
 Any user of the pool can end a pooled stake, once finished its stake period.
 No emergency end stakes during any pool-staking period, bulletproof your patience in the power of a pool.
 Exit an unstaked pool at your leisure - bear in mind the 1% fee.
 Withdraw your staked coins + interest anytime post stake end with zero-fees, pools remain open until drained of all rewards.
 Refer friends and fill pools faster! Earn 12.5% of the 1% pool entry fee.
 Built-in buddy system, receive up to 25% of the previous pool entry fee in HEX direct to your wallet.
 Each pool entry mints bonus  POOL tokens at a 1:100 ratio for HEX entered before fees.
 A 1% burn rate is applied to every  POOL token transfer. Upon reaching the max supply of 100B, minting ceases to exist for permanent burn!
 Freezing capabilities are baked into the  POOL smart-contract, watch this space and utilize your  POOL tokens in future DApps.
