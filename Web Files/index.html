 About

HEXPOOL enables anyone to benefit from the Bigger Pays Better(BPB) HEX staking bonus!

 Pool funds together as one stake to maximize staking profits.
 Pools are staked automatically upon reaching a threshold of 150M HEX(MAX BPB), any overflow funds are rolled over into a new pool.
 Each pool has a set stake length of 36, 365 or 3650 days(MAX LPB), enter any or all pools as many times as you want!
 Any user of the pool can end a pooled stake, once finished its stake period.
 No emergency end stakes during any pool-staking period, bulletproof your patience in the power of a pool.
 Exit an unstaked pool at your leisure - bear in mind the 1% fee on pool entry.
 Withdraw your staked coins + interest anytime post stake end, pools remain open until drained of all rewards.
 Refer friends and fill pools faster! Earn 25% of the pool entry fee.
 Each pool entry mints bonus  POOL tokens at a 1:100 ratio for HEX entered.
